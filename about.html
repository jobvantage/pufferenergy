About

Who we are

Shaping Your Future with Online Banking

PufferPay is a forward-thinking payment solutions provider and agency banking partner committed to making financial services accessible, secure, and effortless. We combine innovative technology with deep industry expertise to help businesses, agents, and everyday users move money with confidence.

At PufferPay, we believe that payments should be simple—no barriers, no friction, no limits. Our platform is built to serve a diverse ecosystem: merchants who need reliable transaction processing, agents who deliver essential financial services to their communities, and customers who expect speed, transparency, and trust.

We exist to bridge the gap between traditional finance and the emerging digital economy, offering seamless tools that help people and businesses thrive.

Our Mission

To simplify financial transactions and expand access to inclusive, technology-driven payment services for individuals, businesses, and communities.

Our Vision

A world where financial services are effortless, connected, and available to everyone—anytime, anywhere.

Our Values

  • Trust: We build technology you can rely on.
  • Innovation: We continually push boundaries to improve how people and businesses transact.
  • Inclusivity: We believe financial access should be universal.
  • Service: We put our users and agent partners at the center of everything we do.
